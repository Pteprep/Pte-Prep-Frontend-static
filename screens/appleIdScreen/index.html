Other login methods (Apple ID)

If you created your apeuni account with WeChat or Apple ID, you can bind your email address to your account in APEUni iOS / Android APP, and use the email address to log in.

(The email binding feature is only available in the latest APEUni APP version -- 4.8.0 or later)

Email-binding process

1、Log in to PTE APEUni iOS / Android App with WeChat or Apple ID (only available in iOS), and enter settings from the "Me" page

2、Click the button "Click to add" to enter the page of email-binding

3、Type the your email address in the dialog box; set login password (for email login); and click "Send code to email"; then type the verification code you receive in email.

4、After successfully binding your email, you can log in to Apeuni's official website / APPs (both iOS and Android) via the newly bound email address.
