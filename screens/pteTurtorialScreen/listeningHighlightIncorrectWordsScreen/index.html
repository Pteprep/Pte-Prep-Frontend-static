STUDYGUIDE

Highlight Incorrect Words

About

Test takers will usually hear a audio clip of 15 to 50 seconds, and see a textual passage, before required to highlight the wrongly pronounced words according to the audio clip. This question type usually consists of 2 or 3 tasks.

Tips

This question type is the easiest one in the Listening Section and accounts for a very considerable proportion in terms of point. No matter what goal is set for PTE score, make sure to get a full mark in this question type, which is very likely to achieve through practice within a short period of time.

Time Span

Despite HIW’s significance in terms of points in the Listening Section, do not spend too much time in it, 2 minutes at most for each task.

Practice Goal

WHV: Make sure to get a full mark here.

50 points: Practice on Apeuni website with 1.2* speed and get a full mark at a time.

65 points: Practice on Apeuni website with 1.2* speed and get a full mark at a time.

79 points: Practice on Apeuni website with 1.2* speed and get a full mark at a time.

Practice Task

Daily practice 10 HIW tasks on Apeuni website( with 1.2* speed, then gradually upgraded to 1.5* speed); keep practicing one task until a full mark at a time can be achieved before go to next task.
