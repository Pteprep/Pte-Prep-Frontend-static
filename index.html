PTE Academic Guide

What is the meaning of PTE?

The full form of PTE is the Pearson Test of English, normally referring to the academic version. It is also known as PTE-A, Pearson Academic, or Pearson Test of English Academic. PTE is a computer-based exam. It also provides online mock tests on Pearson PTE's website.

Read Aloud exam tips

PTE Reading Guide

Multiple Choice Single Answer exam tips

The Reading Multiple Choice Single Answer (MCS) question type accounts for only a very small proportion of marks in the overall Reading score. The most important technique for MCS is time management. Please complete each question of this type within 1 or 2 minutes. Otherwise, you will not have enough time for the more important FIB questions.
